Creamy Mac and Cheese Recipe

Mac and cheese is the ultimate comfort food, loved by both children and adults. It uses a combination of cheeses, layered in the dish as well as melted into a rich and creamy cheese sauce, for the ultimate cheesiness and deliciousness!



Ingredients

  • 450g uncooked shells/macaroni pasta
  • 4 cups whole/fresh milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Procedure

  1. Use a large pot of salted water and bring it to a boil. Add the pasta and cook it just until al dente.
  2. Prepare a medium saucepan, set the stove in medium heat. Pour milk and warm it until it is simmering. Turn off heat and set it aside.
  3. In a pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Whisk in the flour and cook the mixture, whisking constantly, until it turns light brown in color and has a slightly toasted aroma, for about 3 minutes.
  4. Turn off heat, slowly whisk the warm milk into the flour mixture, and continue whisking to avoid any lumps. The mixture will become very thick and then thin out gradually until it is smooth.
  5. Return the pot to the stove over medium high heat and cook the mixture while whisking constantly until it has thickened enough to coat the back of a spoon, about 2 minutes.
  6. Season the mixture with salt and pepper and reduce the heat to medium. Stir in the cheddar and parmessan cheese until they are melted and the cheese sauce is smooth.
  7. Add pasta, stirring to combine, and cook just until the pasta is warmed throughout.

Your creamy mac and cheese is ready. Bon appetit!
